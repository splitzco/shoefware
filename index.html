About

shoëfware is a software engineering firm that designs and builds commerce engines, clinical tools, and human-centered platforms from first principles — purpose-built for the industries they serve.

Engineered Platforms
01
SplitzCo™
Hyperlocal Commerce

A franchise-route delivery platform built on the SplitzCo Commerce Engine (SCE) — powering 15-minute local delivery and next-day fulfillment to any US address. B2C, B2B, mobile, and operator tools, all in one engine.

02
Forge™
Law Enforcement Platform

A law enforcement resource platform built as a shared officer record engine — powering SafetyStream (MCOLES compliance and training) as its first application, with scheduling, wellness, equipment, and incident management on the roadmap.

03
DDM™
Healthcare Data Management

The Doyen Data Model — an electronic blueprint that maps how data moves across a healthcare organization. Contracts, interfaces, process flows, disaster plans, training models: everything a CIO needs to make decisions, unified in one place.

04
CompanionBridge™
Senior Technology

A human-centered companion platform designed for older adults — reducing isolation and increasing connection through intuitive, accessible technology built around real human needs.
